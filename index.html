About Me

Hi, this is Frank!

I'm a Data Scientist and Data-Driven storyteller based on Washington D.C. with previous experience in consulting. I am passionate to wrestle with complex data and apply machine learning to tell stories and solve real-world business problems.

I will soon graduate from my Master of Science in Data Science (MSDS) at the George Washington University, where I have developed a strong programming and statistics skill set that can tackle business problems involving big data.
